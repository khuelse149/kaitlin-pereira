NOAA WINGS Fellow 2025–2026

I study winter wave dynamics in the Great Lakes to improve operational weather forecasting. My research uses WAVEWATCH III numerical wave modeling, NOAA buoy observations, and NASA SWOT satellite data to better understand how wind, waves, and surface roughness interact in large lakes — and how to represent those processes more accurately in forecast models that protect coastal communities.
